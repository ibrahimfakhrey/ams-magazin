    Five Reasons To Study Science

    By : Fawzy Ali Fawzy

    First reason: science helps solve problems Science is about finding solutions to extremely complicated problems. This can also be studying what possible solutions have already been suggested, or ways in which we can attempt to find these solutions.

    Second reason: science helps us discover new things about our world Science helps us to learn about our world and know-how every living thing lives and also help us to know the evolution of humans and the new inventions and technology.

    Third reason: science encourages creativity In science, we try a lot of new experiments that level up our creativity level and let us want to try new things. Look around you, all of the technology around you is made by creative people who tried a lot until they got the results we see now.

    Fourth reason: improve your analytical skills Science teaches you several valuable skills, but being able to analyze a large amount of information concisely is important for all disciplines. Indeed, the ability to conclude from evidence is essential and will allow you to be precise.

    Fifth reason: keep your options open for college Choosing only three or four subjects is often difficult, especially for students who feel torn between the arts and sciences. However, by choosing one or two science subjects, you will keep both doors open for your future degree.
